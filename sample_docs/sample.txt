The ONE Thing is a productivity idea based on focusing on the most important task. The focusing question is: What's the ONE thing I can do such that by doing it everything else will be easier or unnecessary?

The main idea is that extraordinary results come from narrowing your focus. Instead of trying to do everything, you should identify the highest-impact task and give it your best time and energy.

Multitasking reduces focus and can make important work slower and less effective. A person should choose their most important task by asking which action will create the biggest positive result.
